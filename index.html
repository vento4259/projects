Welcome to Net X Computers

Net X is a full service IT Company based in Centerville Ohio. We specialize in servicing small business IT repair and maintenance along with home user IT repair and virus removal. We even have an in house team of professionals to help you setup your website and structure your marketing strategy! We pride ourselves in being your one stop shop for all of your IT and marketing needs! We tailor our service to you and your needs while answering each and every question you have to the best of our ability. In today’s fast paced society we understand the need to have technology functioning properly when you need it most! We offer the turnaround times of 48-72 hours on most of our home user software repairs as well as small business visits within 1-2 hours. We understand the importance of technology and it is our mission to always have you up and running at your full capacity!
